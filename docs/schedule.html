Daily Walking

Every day — in addition to strength sessions

Goal
Brisk pace — fast enough to feel it, slow enough to talk
20–40 min is enough — longer on rest days if you feel good
No phone, no headphones — let it be mental recovery too
Lunch walk (1pm) works well as a daily anchor
Why it works for BP
Daily moderate cardio is one of the most effective BP interventions
Consistency matters more than intensity — same time daily is ideal
Walking after meals (especially dinner) lowers post-meal BP spikes
If morning BP is above 140/90, skip strength that day and walk only
